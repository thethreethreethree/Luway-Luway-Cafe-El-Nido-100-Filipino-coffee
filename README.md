Luway Luway Cafe El Nido 100% Filipino coffee

Luway Luway Cafe El Nido 100% Filipino coffee is a small, independent café located along C. Hama Street in Barangay Buena Suerte, El Nido, Palawan, Philippines. It highlights locally sourced Filipino coffee beans and plant-based, traveler-friendly fare in a relaxed tropical setting. The café has become a well-loved breakfast and brunch spot for visitors exploring El Nido’s beaches and island tours.

Atmosphere and Concept
The café blends island charm with a minimalist, bohemian aesthetic. Its laid-back vibe attracts both backpackers and digital nomads seeking good coffee, fresh food, and friendly local service. The space offers indoor and limited outdoor seating, encouraging slow mornings and community connection over coffee.

Menu and Coffee
Luway Luway’s menu centers on Filipino-grown coffee served as espresso-based drinks, caramel lattes, and iced specialties. Visitors often praise its smoothie bowls, double-egg breakfast sandwiches, vegan burgers, and house-made cashew milk alternatives. Desserts like coconut ice cream and banana chocolate bread add a local touch. Breakfast and brunch are served until mid-afternoon. 

Service and Reputation
Known for cheerful staff and consistent quality, the café earns strong online ratings—around 4.9 out of 5 on Google—placing it among El Nido’s top coffee spots. Customers frequently commend its professional service, fair pricing (₱200–₱400 per person), and cozy ambience ideal for breakfast or midday breaks between tours. 

Sustainability and Local Pride
Emphasizing 100% Filipino coffee supports Palawan’s emerging specialty coffee scene. Its use of cashew-based milk and vegan options reflects a growing eco-conscious trend in El Nido’s culinary community.
