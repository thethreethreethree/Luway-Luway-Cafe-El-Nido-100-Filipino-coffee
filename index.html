Our Story

Where Filipino Coffee Finds Its Home

"Ano ang heritage mo?"

Nestled along C. Hama Street in Barangay Buena Suerte, Luway Luway is more than a cafe — it's a celebration of Filipino heritage. We source 100% locally grown Philippine coffee beans, support Palawan's emerging specialty coffee scene, and serve each cup with island warmth and pride.

Our menu is built around honest, nourishing food — smoothie bowls, vegan burgers, double-egg breakfast sandwiches, and house-made cashew milk — all crafted with the traveler, the local, and the planet in mind.
